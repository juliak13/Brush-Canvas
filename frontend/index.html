About Brush & Canvas

Brush & Canvas is an online learning platform focused on creative arts education. Our system was designed as a database systems project to manage courses, student enrollments, certificates, and artwork submissions.

We researched modern learning platforms and university course structures to create an accessible experience for beginner-level students interested in creative arts.
